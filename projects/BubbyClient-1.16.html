BubbyClient-Fabric-1.16

Best client

repo

About

By far the best Minecraft Fabric 1.16 utility mod. One of the only to have a combat module to calculate crystal damage and place.

Install

  1. Clone the repo: git clone https://github.com/BubbyRoosh1/BubbyClient-Fabric-1.16
  2. Enter the directory: cd BubbyClient-Fabric-1.16
  3. Build the project: ./gradlew build
  4. Put the .jar file in your mods folder: mv build/libs/BubbyClient-Final.jar ~/.minecraft/mods/

Modules

  • AirJump: Allows you to jump while in the air
  • Ambiance: Allows the player to control the time of day/weather (clientside)
  • Announcer: Annoyance
  • AntiBlind: Removes the Blindness effect
  • AntiChunkBan: Prevents chunk bans with bad packets
  • AntiForceLook: Prevents the server from forcing your position
  • AntiHunger: Reduces the hunger of the player
  • AntiHurtcam: Prevents the hurtcam effect
  • AntiLevitate: Removes the Levitation effect
  • AntiSlow: Removes the Slowness effect
  • AutoCrystal: Calculates the best position to place an ender crystal to damage the target entity and places it
  • AutoDisconnect: Disconnects the player if certain conditions are met
  • AutoMine: Automatically.. mines..
  • AutoRespawn: Automatically makes the player respawn
  • AutoSprint: Automatically makes the player sprint
  • AutoTotem: Automatically puts a Totem of Undying the the player’s offhand
  • AutoWalk: Automatically makes the player walk
  • BlockOutline: Renders an outline against the block the player is looking at
  • BowSpam: Spams arrows when right click is pressed
  • ChatSuffix: Puts a suffix at the end of each chat message sent
  • ChestESP: Renders boxes around chests; visible through walls
  • Criticals: Forces critical hits
  • CrystalAura: Attacks any ender crystals within range
  • ElytraFly: Better movement with elytra
  • ElytraToggle: Automatically toggles elytra/chestplate
  • ESP: Draws a box around selected entities; visible through walls
  • FakePlayer: Creates a fake player
  • FancyChat: Alters each chat message sent
  • FastFall: Makes the player fall faster
  • FastUse: Allows the player to interact faster
  • Fly: Allows the player to fly
  • Freecam: Allows the player to move only clientside (with options for flight and noclip)
  • Fullbright: Makes the world brighter
  • GreenText: Puts ‘>’ at the beginning of each message sent
  • HoleESP: Renders boxes around holes that are fit for Crystal PVP; visible through walls
  • HoleFill: Fills nearby holes that are fit for Crystal PVP
  • XCarry: Allows items to be carried in the crafting slots, even when the inventory is closed
  • IRC: Connects you to an IRC Channel
  • Jesus: Allows the player to walk on water
  • Killaura: Automatically attacks selected entities
  • Liquids: Allows the player to place blocks on top of liquids
  • NameTags: Better nametags
  • NoClip: Allows the player to go through things
  • NoFall: Prevents fall damage
  • AntiFire: Prevents the fire effect from rendering
  • NoSwing: Prevents the swing animation serverside
  • PopbobLag: spams “lag” in chat
  • PopCounter: Counts players’ totem pops
  • PortalESP: Renders boxes around nether portals; visible through walls
  • PortalGodMode: Puts the player in “GodMode”, preventing any damage when going through nether portals.
  • Portals: Allows the player to open GUI in nether portals
  • Spammer: Spams the contents of a .txt file into chat
  • Speed: Allows the player to move faster
  • SpeedMine: Allows the player to mine faster
  • Step: Allows the player to step higher
  • Surround: Places blocks around the player’s feet, making it a suitable hole for Crystal PVP
  • Timer: Speeds of Minecraft’s tick duration (clientside)
  • Tracers: Draws lines to selected entities; visible through walls
  • Velocity: Prevents knockback
